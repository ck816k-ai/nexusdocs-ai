Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Free tier work?

You get 3 full document analyses per month. Each full analysis (Summary + Risks) uses 2 credits, and each follow-up question uses 1 credit.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes. You can change your plan or switch to pay-as-you-go at any time from your account dashboard.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We do not store your documents. Everything is processed temporarily and deleted after analysis.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards via Stripe. No long-term contracts required.
